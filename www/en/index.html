Who are we?

"KPIndustries" deals with digital scheming and blueprints development. "KPIndustries" will sketch any kind of objects just for You. Those CAD files are compatible with all kinds of computerized, object making machines, such as: lathe machines, 3D routers, milling machines, 3D printers, cutting lasers etc.

How do we work?

After You have developed the part or object you want in your head, you can just simply draw it on a piece of paper and write some dimensions on that same sketch. Take a picture of it and send it to our e-mail, which You will find in the |Contacts| section. If you don't want to draw or sketch you can always contact us and we will send a team to design the part you need and take proper and accurate measurements.
